Hey, I'm Yashveer.

I compiled this whole thing because I'm a big believer in open data. Over four-five years of working with Indian geospatial data, I kept seeing people redo the same work — spending weeks just finding and collating boundary files, census geometries, and electoral maps from scattered government portals.

My belief is simple: if I've already spent the time, the next person shouldn't have to. So here it is — every dataset I could find, organized in one place, ready to download. Whether you're a researcher, a policy analyst, a student, or just someone building something cool with Indian geo data, I hope this saves you some time.
