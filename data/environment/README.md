# Environmental / Natural Boundary Data

Boundary datasets for natural and environmentally regulated areas across India.

## Sub-datasets

| Directory | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| `forests/` | Forest administrative and reserved forest boundaries |
| `coastal/` | Coastal Regulation Zone (CRZ) boundaries and regulation lines |
| `landuse/` | Land use classification data |

## Sources

- Forest Survey of India (FSI)
- Bharatmaps / Parivesh (MoEFCC)
- PM GatiShakti National Master Plan
- National Centre for Sustainable Coastal Management (NCSCM)
- Survey of India (SOI)
- National Centre for Geo-informatics (NCOG)
