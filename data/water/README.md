# Water Data

Water resource and hydrological geospatial datasets for India — rivers, lakes, reservoirs, wetlands, irrigation infrastructure, hydrological boundaries, and more.

## Sub-datasets

| Directory | Description | Source |
|-----------|-------------|--------|
| `rivers/` | Rivers, streams, and water channels (polyline & polygon) | India-WRIS, Survey of India, NCOG |
| `waterbodies/` | Lakes, reservoirs, tanks, and ponds (polygon & point) | India-WRIS, Survey of India, Amrit Sarovar |
| `hydro-boundaries/` | River basins, sub-basins, watersheds, and micro-watersheds | India-WRIS, SLUSI |
| `irrigation/` | Canals, dams, aqueducts, wells, weirs, and other irrigation infrastructure | Survey of India, BharatMaps, India-WRIS |
| `wetlands/` | Wetlands, Ramsar sites, salt pans, and swamps | BharatMaps/Parivesh, Survey of India, NCOG |
| `waterbody-census/` | National Water Body Census point locations | Jal Dharohar (Ministry of Jal Shakti) |
| `urban-water/` | Urban stormwater drains, ghats, and water bodies | SBM, AMRUT |
| `natural/` | Springs, waterfalls, geothermal springs, and islands | Survey of India, Bhuvan, NGDR |

## Sources

- **India-WRIS** — Water Resources Information System (Ministry of Jal Shakti)
- **Survey of India** — Topographic maps (water features)
- **NCOG** — National Centre of Geo-Informatics (BharatMaps)
- **Amrit Sarovar Water Observatory** — Pond monitoring (Ministry of Jal Shakti)
- **SLUSI** — Soil and Land Use Survey of India (watershed boundaries)
- **PM GatiShakti / BharatMaps** — Canals, dams, and irrigation
- **Parivesh** — MoEFCC wetland and Ramsar boundaries
- **Jal Dharohar** — National Water Body Census (Ministry of Jal Shakti)
- **Swachh Bharat Mission / AMRUT** — Urban water infrastructure
- **ISRO Bhuvan** — Islands and natural features
- **NGDR (Bhukosh)** — Geological Survey of India geothermal springs
