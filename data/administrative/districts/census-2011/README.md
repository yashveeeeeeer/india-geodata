# District Boundaries -- Census 2011

District boundaries from the Census of India 2011. Shapefile format.

- **Feature count:** 640 districts
- **Format:** Shapefile (`2011_Dist.shp` and associated files)
- **Source:** Census of India via DataMeet
- **Coordinate system:** Geographic (WGS 84)
