# Survey of India Outline Maps

Outline maps of India from the Survey of India. Includes PDF format maps at various scales
and a vector shapefile version in the `india-outline-vector/` subdirectory.

- **PDF maps:** Multiple outline maps with and without rivers at various scales
- **Vector version:** See [india-outline-vector/](india-outline-vector/)
- **Source:** Survey of India

See the [repository README](../../../README.md) for full documentation.
